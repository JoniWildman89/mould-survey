Mould Detection Survey

Fungi can be used to create sustainable insulation materials by growing them on organic substrates such as hemp or wood chips. As the fungi grow, they naturally bind the material together, acting as a biological adhesive. Once fully grown, the material is dried in an oven to kill the fungus, resulting in a durable, bio-based product.

However, these types of materials—along with other natural construction materials like straw—can be vulnerable to unwanted mould growth. This study aims to monitor and measure mould development using image analysis.
